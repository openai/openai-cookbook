## Scope of this Release

This release serves as a **public, timestamped record** documenting the following observed events:

- Repeated deletion and reinitialisation of a researcher account without authorised action  
- Removal of biography, institutional affiliations, and previously uploaded research materials  
- Alteration of workflow states and version histories  
- Reappearance of protocols with modified metadata  
- Removal or suppression of institutional affiliations, including internationally recognised academic institutions  

All observations are documented with contemporaneous evidence, including screenshots, attachment inventories, timestamps, and platform state indicators.
