**17 U.S.C. § 501 – Copyright Infringement**  
Defines the exclusive rights of the copyright owner and establishes that any unlicensed use, reproduction, or distribution of protected works constitutes infringement.  
Source: https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/501

**18 U.S.C. § 1030 – Computer Fraud and Abuse Act**  
Prohibits unauthorized access, data tampering, theft of software or misuse of protected technological systems.  
Source: https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1030

**15 U.S.C. § 78j(b) – Securities Fraud**  
Forbids deceptive or manipulative practices in connection with the purchase or sale of securities.  
Source: https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/78j
