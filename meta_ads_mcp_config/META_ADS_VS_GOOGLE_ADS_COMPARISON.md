## 🚀 **IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP**

### **Phase 1: Setup (Week 1)**
1. **Create Meta Ads Account** and verify business
2. **Install Meta Pixel** on Colppy website
3. **Set up conversion events** (trial sign-ups, lead generation)
4. **Create custom audiences** (website visitors, customer list)
5. **Design ad creatives** (images, videos, copy)

### **Phase 2: Launch (Week 2)**
1. **Create campaigns** with defined structure
2. **Set up UTM tracking** for HubSpot integration
3. **Launch with conservative budgets** ($75,000 ARS/day)
4. **Monitor initial performance** and make adjustments
5. **Set up automated reporting** and alerts

### **Phase 3: Optimize (Week 3-4)**
1. **Analyze performance data** and identify winners
2. **Reallocate budgets** to top-performing ad sets
3. **Test new ad creatives** and messaging
4. **Refine audience targeting** based on data
5. **Scale successful campaigns** gradually

### **Phase 4: Scale (Month 2+)**
1. **Increase budgets** on winning campaigns
2. **Expand to new audiences** and interests
3. **Test new campaign objectives** (app installs, events)
4. **Integrate with other channels** (email, SMS)
5. **Develop advanced automation** and bidding strategies
